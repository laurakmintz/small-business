CULTURAL PROGRAMMING FOR THE ARTS COMMUNITY

Featured Artworks for Sale

Founded in 2012, The Committee focuses on producing unique experiences for the arts community. Exploring the constantly blurring line between art, fashion, design and music, The Committee is dedicated to bringing contrasting individuals or groups together to expand the boundaries within each field. Through artist studio visits, gallery talks, and pop-up exhibitions, The Committee's goal is to make the arts more accessible to New York City's young professionals.
